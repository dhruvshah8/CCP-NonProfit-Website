Who We Are

CCP is a one day leadership event for 100+ underprivileged children part of the Boys and Girls Club of Peel to experience the joy of Christmas. This organization aims to enhance the lives of young children as well as provide them with opportunities to become leaders. A range of trustworthy youth volunteers will help facilitate the conference and cultivate an open atmosphere for the children attending.

This year, CCP is scheduled for Sunday December 15th, 2019.
